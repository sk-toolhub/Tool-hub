Frequently Asked Questions

What is image compression? +

Image compression reduces the file size of an image while maintaining its visual quality. This helps images load faster on websites, use less storage space, and upload quicker to social media or email.

Will compression reduce image quality? +

Our tool uses smart compression algorithms that minimize quality loss. You can adjust the quality slider to find the perfect balance between file size and image quality for your needs.

What image formats are supported? +

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. These are the most common image formats used on websites and social media platforms.

Is there a file size limit? +

No, there's no file size limit. Our tool processes images directly in your browser, so you can compress images of any size.

Do you keep my images? +

No, we don't store or save any of your images. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy.

Why should I compress images? +

Compressed images load faster on websites (improving SEO), take less storage space, upload quicker to social media, and send faster via email.
