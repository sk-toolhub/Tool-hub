What is an Image to Text Tool?

An Image to Text tool, also known as OCR software, uses advanced algorithms to recognize characters within images and convert them into machine-readable text. Unlike basic text recognition, modern OCR tools like ours can handle various fonts, sizes, and even some handwriting styles with impressive accuracy.

Tips for Best OCR Results

To maximize text extraction accuracy:

  • Use clear, high-contrast images with good lighting
  • Ensure text is horizontal and not at extreme angles
  • Crop images to focus on the text area before uploading
  • For printed text, use at least 300 DPI resolution scans
  • Avoid complex backgrounds when possible
