Tips for Best Results

  • Use clear, high-contrast images for maximum accuracy
  • Ensure text is horizontal and not at extreme angles
  • Crop images to focus on the text area
  • For printed text, use images with at least 300 DPI resolution
  • Avoid complex backgrounds when possible
