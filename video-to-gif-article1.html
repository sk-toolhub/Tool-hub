How to Convert Video to GIF Online – Complete Guide

Published: Dec 2, 2025 • Reading time: 6 min

Intro: GIFs are great for short animations. In this guide you will learn how to convert your video files into high-quality GIFs using free online tools on SK Toolhub.

Step 1 — Choose the right video

Use short clips (3–6 seconds) with limited motion for smaller GIF size. Prefer MP4 format for better compatibility.

Step 2 — Trim the clip (optional)

Trim to the exact segment you need. Smaller duration = smaller GIF. SK Toolhub's Video Trimmer helps here.

Step 3 — Convert using the Video to GIF tool

Upload the trimmed clip and choose: frame rate (10–20 fps), resolution (480px width for small gifs), and color options if available.

Tips for best results

  • Lower FPS reduces size but smoothness drops — 12–15 fps is a good balance.
  • Reduce colors if file size is a problem.
