Frequently Asked Questions

How does PDF compression work? +

PDF compression works by optimizing various elements within the PDF file including images, fonts, and metadata. Our tool uses advanced algorithms to remove unnecessary data, compress images intelligently, and optimize the file structure while maintaining the visual quality of your document.

Will compression reduce PDF quality? +

Our tool uses intelligent compression that minimizes quality loss. For text-based PDFs, you can achieve up to 90% size reduction with virtually no quality loss. For image-heavy PDFs, you can adjust the quality slider to find the perfect balance between file size and image quality.

What's the maximum file size I can compress? +

You can compress PDF files up to 50MB in size. Since all processing happens in your browser, there are no server-side limitations. For larger files, consider splitting them into smaller parts or using desktop software.

Are my files secure? Do you store them? +

100% secure! All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, are not uploaded to any server, and are not stored anywhere. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive documents.

Why should I compress PDF files? +

Compressed PDFs are easier to share via email (many email providers have 25MB limits), upload faster to websites, take less storage space, load quicker in web browsers, and are more convenient for mobile users with limited data plans.

What types of PDF can be compressed? +

Our tool works with all standard PDF files including text documents, scanned documents, image-based PDFs, forms, presentations, and eBooks. It preserves text selectability, hyperlinks, and form fields during compression.
