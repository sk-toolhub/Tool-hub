What Is a QR Code and Barcode?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional code that stores information such as URLs, text, contact details, or payment links. Barcodes, on the other hand, are one-dimensional codes commonly used in retail, logistics, and inventory systems to represent product data.

Both QR codes and barcodes can be scanned using a camera-enabled device. Our QR and barcode scanner online supports popular formats and ensures accurate decoding in real time.
