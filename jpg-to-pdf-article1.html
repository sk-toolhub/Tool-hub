Method 2: Desktop Software Solutions

For regular conversions or working with sensitive documents, desktop software offers more control and privacy.

2 Using Adobe Acrobat Pro (Professional Grade)

  1. Open Adobe Acrobat Pro DC
  2. Click "Tools" > "Create PDF"
  3. Select your JPG files
  4. Adjust conversion settings (compression, resolution)
  5. Click "Create"
  6. Save your PDF file

3 Using Microsoft Word (Built-in Method)

  1. Open Microsoft Word
  2. Insert your JPG images into a document
  3. Arrange and format as needed
  4. Go to "File" > "Export"
  5. Choose "Create PDF/XPS Document"
  6. Select quality options and click "Publish"
