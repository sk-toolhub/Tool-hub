When to Convert Image Formats?

For Websites

Convert to WebP for faster loading and better SEO performance.

For Print

Convert to PNG or TIFF for high-quality printing with no compression artifacts.

For Social Media

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert? +

You can convert between JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP formats. Our tool supports all major image formats used on the web and in print.

Will converting reduce image quality? +

Our converter preserves maximum quality during format conversion. For lossy formats like JPG, you can adjust the quality slider to control file size vs quality balance.

Can I convert multiple images at once? +

Currently, our tool processes one image at a time. For batch conversion, you can convert multiple images sequentially.

Is WebP better than JPG/PNG? +

WebP provides 25-35% smaller file sizes than JPG with similar quality, and supports transparency like PNG. It's the recommended format for modern websites.

Do you keep my images? +

No, all image processing happens in your browser. We don't store, save, or upload your images to any server.

Why convert PNG to JPG? +

Convert PNG to JPG when you need smaller file sizes for web use, email attachments, or social media. JPG files are typically 5-10x smaller than equivalent PNGs.
